A private preview of the guest gesture — trace the sign of the cross in the air to awaken the font.

Your position

Stand about arm's length from the screen. Keep your signing hand at chest height — where you'd naturally trace the cross.

The four touches

  • 1 Up — heaven
  • 2 Down — earth
  • 3 Left — across your chest
  • 4 Right — complete the sign

On site

This preview uses your webcam. At the rooftop Font, touch points will be calibrated to the mounted camera and where guests stand — typically a 15-minute remote setup once hardware is installed.

Ready when you are
